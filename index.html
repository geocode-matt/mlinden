About Me

Welcome to my portfolio page!

I am a Full Stack Web Developer. I am actively seeking employment as a Web/Software Developer in the DFW, Houston, or Austin, Texas areas.

Background: I began the web development program through UT Austin with the original intention of applying these skills to improve workflows and processes at the company I was working for (Rio Oil and Gas II). Economic hardships brought about by Covid-19 led to the collapse of Rio but, refreshingly, has reignited my passion for programming and led me to a total dedication to becoming a full-time developer. My aspiration is to utilize the foundational technical and problem-solving skills I have developed during my ~10 years as a geoscientist to pursue a career in full stack web development.

Languages => Javascript, HTML, CSS, Python
Libraries & Technologies => JQuery, Bootstrap, Foundation, JSON, AJAX, NPM, MySQL, MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js
Editors & Version Control => Visual Studio Code, Git/GitHub
Other => Responsive Design, Agile Development Best Practices
